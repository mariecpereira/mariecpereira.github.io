About

Mariana is a PhD candidate in cognitive neuroscience investigating the brain networks that characterize rapid-eye movement (REM) sleep. She is currently pursueing her doctoral degree in the Radboud University Medical Centre, at the Donders Institute in Nijmegen, the Netherlands, under the supervision of Dr. Martin Dresler.

Mariana's academic publications can be found online.
